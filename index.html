Hi there, I'm Graham.

I'm a 42 year old Australian living in Chiang Mai Thailand.

I live in the district area called Saraphi in a village just outside of the city and I have been living here for a little over two years. I really love life here in Thailand, the food, the people, Thai culture and the lifestyle it brings. I intend to remain living here permanently.

When I first arrived in Thailand I decided on doing a Thai language course so I could communicate better with the locals in my village. I studied the language for six months and found the language challenging to learn, but I did manage to learn enough to be able to communicate in simple Thai.
